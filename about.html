Tessa Netting (born September 1st, 1990) is an actress, YouTuber, host, and content-creator. She began her professional career playing Susan Parks in the Original Broadway Cast of Billy Elliot the Musical where she performed in over 1,000 shows. Since moving to Los Angeles, she has been seen and heard in numerous voice over roles, commercials, web series, and television shows, most notably playing Hazel in the Disney Channel show BUNK'D. She also has recently branched out into audiobook narration and won the 2020 Earphones Award for her work on Redwood and Ponytail. Online, you can find her promoting Harry Potter on YouTube and cosplaying on Instagram. She has been featured in Forbes, New York Magazine, Huffington Post, CBS, NBC, Playbill, and Billboard. In 2018, Tessa created the podcast Fantastic Geeks (And Where To Find Them) with her fellow voice actor and content-creator Anna Brisbin to discuss a variety of fandoms.
